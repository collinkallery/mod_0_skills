How to Become a Turing Student
1. Apply to Turing
  1.1 Choose one of two programs (FEE or BEE)
  1.2 Fill out online application
    1.2.1 Basic identity and contact information
      1.2.1.1 Enter first and last name
      1.2.1.2 Enter home address
      1.2.1.3 Enter phone number
      1.2.1.4 Enter email address
      1.2.1.5 Enter age, birthday
    1.2.2 Work history
      1.2.2.1 Enter last two to three employment positions
      1.2.2.2 Enter employment contact information
      1.2.2.3 Enter skills used during employment
      1.2.2.4 Enter degrees, diplomas, certificates, etc.
    1.2.3 Logic quiz
      1.2.3.1 Pull out notepad and pen
      1.2.3.2 Read question
      1.2.3.3 Answer question
      1.2.3.4 Repeat until all 8 questions are finished
    1.2.4 Submit online application
2. Interview Process
  2.1 Receive email with interview offer
    2.1.1 Accept or deny
    2.1.2 Choose interview time and date
  2.2 Go to Interview
    2.2.1 Put on nice interview outfit
    2.2.2 Drive to Turing
  2.3 Interview
    2.3.1 Meet with interviewee
    2.3.2 Sit down
    2.3.3 Answer questions of interviewee
    2.3.4 Ask any questions you may have
  2.4 Wait for offer
    2.4.1 Check email daily
    2.4.2 Open offer email
    2.4.3 Accept offer
    2.4.4 Choose cohort
    2.4.5 Pay $1,200 deposit
    2.4.6 Pick up computer
3. Pre-work
  3.1 Mod 0
    3.1.1 Choose Mod 0 cohort
    3.1.2 Download ZOOM
    3.1.3 Download SLACK
    3.1.4 Meet on zoom each Tuesday and Thursday
    3.1.5 Complete weekly homework
  3.2 Capstone
    3.2.1 Professional development
      3.2.1.1 Pairin Survey
      3.2.1.2 Interpret Survey
      3.2.1.3 Respond to questions
      3.2.1.4 Upload screenshot of results
    3.2.2 Technical
      3.2.2.1 Buy books
      3.2.2.2 Complete days 0 - 7 on github
